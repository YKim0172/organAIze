OrganAIze

OrganAIze, the best tool to help you find the right Computer Science courses for you!

Computer Science is a broad field with many specialties (e.g., Machine Learning, Networking, Cryptography). Often times, students want to learn more about a specific specialty but may not know the courses that best suit their interests. Sometimes, students may want to build an application, but might be unaware of the relevant topics they need to learn. However, with OrganAIse, students can discover the courses that will help them learn the skills they need.

Using OrganAIse can be done in 4 simple steps:

Step 1: Create Your Account

Create your account and tell us what courses you have already taken so far. Making an account is fast and takes less than 5 minutes!

Step 2: Tell us what you want to learn.

When you login to your account, type into the search box anything you want to learn about. You can type in "I want to learn about web development" or "How do I make a game where multiple different machines can play together".

Step 3: See your recommend courses.

You will see several courses that you haven't taken yet that align with your response in Step 2. Not only will you see the best courses for you in order of relevance, you'll also see the average GPA and hours spent for each class to better understand the difficulty and time commitment that's required.

Step 4: Update us on the courses you've taken and search again.

Let us know what courses you've finished by editing your profile. Then go back to step 2 and repeat the process from there, and every future search will take into account the courses you've just told us you've taken.
